Code Her World

Code Her World is run by students for students. Our mission is to encourage young girls to pursue a career in computer science. We hope to inspire our students so that one day, they can break the gender gap in STEM and change the world through technology! ⚡️💻. Partnering with Why Hate Math

Code Her World partnered with Why Hate Math, a summer math camp for elementary schoolers in Brooklyn, NY. Our team got to teach students about algorithms using robot mice — and they practiced "coding" them by clicking buttons to program them through mazes and challenges!

FAQ

Who can signup?

Anyone can! Even though we recommend our programs for high schoolers, everyone is welcome!

How much do camps cost?

Nothing! All programs are FREE!

What should I bring?

All you need is a computer and fast internet! You can also keep a notebook with you if you would like.

Do I need any past coding experience?

Absolutely not! All of our programs start with the basic fundamentals of every language, so you will learn everything you need.

Any questions, comments, or concerns? Feel free to reach out to us!
