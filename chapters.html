Chapters

Bring Code Her World to your school or community. Our chapter program empowers students to lead STEM initiatives locally.

What Is a Chapter?

A Code Her World chapter is a student-led club at your school or in your community that hosts workshops, study groups, and coding events for girls interested in computer science. Chapters receive support, resources, and curriculum from our national team to help you make an impact right where you are.

📚

Access Resources

Get workshop materials, slide decks, and coding curricula designed by our instructors — ready to use in your region.

🌎

Build Community

Connect with other chapter leaders nationwide, share ideas, and grow a supportive network of girls in technology.

🌸

Lead & Grow

Develop leadership skills by organizing events, mentoring peers, and representing a 501(c)(3) organization.

Start a Chapter

Interested in starting a Code Her World chapter at your school? We're expanding our chapter network and would love to hear from you. Reach out to learn about requirements, the application process, and how we can support you. Applications will be posted soon and they are year-round!
