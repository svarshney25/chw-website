Past Events

From hackathons to hands-on workshops — here's a look at what Code Her World has brought to students around the world.

Workshop

Networking Panel with Microsoft

We partnered with Microsoft to host a networking panel where students could connect with professionals in the tech industry. Girls all over the area got to learn about careers at Microsoft, and learn about the company's latest technologie

Workshop

Data Science Workshop

Co-founders Shivika and Esha led a multi-day virtual data science workshop teaching students Python fundamentals and how to analyze real-world data. Participants worked hands-on in Google Colab — learning dictionaries, sets, data structures, and core analysis techniques to draw insights from datasets.

Hackathon

Hack the Halls

Code Her World's first-ever hackathon challenged students to build holiday-themed applications over three weeks. With over 200 participants from around the world, the event was a huge success — teams submitted creative websites, games, and apps, and we awarded hundreds of dollars in prizes including Amazon gift cards, Interview Cake licenses, and gen.xyz domains.

Camp

Intro to Web Dev Camp

Our month-long, five-session intro to web development camp taught students the fundamentals of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Meeting once a week over the course of a month, students built real websites from scratch and left with published projects and a strong foundation in frontend development.
