Meet the Team

Code Her World is run by passionate students who believe every girl deserves a place in computer science.

Co-Founders

SV

Shivika Varshney

Co-Founder

Shivika co-founded Code Her World to create free, accessible programming education for girls everywhere. She leads workshops, hackathons, and organizational strategy.

ES

Esha Sehgal

Co-Founder

Esha co-founded Code Her World alongside Shivika with a shared vision of closing the gender gap in STEM. She helps design curriculum and run virtual events for students worldwide.
